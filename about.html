About Ragama Shopping Center

Established in 2023, Ragama Shopping Center was built with a vision to provide a modern, convenient, and exciting shopping experience for the people of Ragama and surrounding areas. We are more than just a place to buy things; we are a community gathering space where friends meet, families celebrate, and memories are made.

With over 50 retail outlets, a world-class cinema, a vibrant food court, and ample parking, we offer everything you need under one roof. Our commitment to quality, cleanliness, and customer service sets us apart.
